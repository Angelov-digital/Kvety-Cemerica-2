Why choose us

We are a team of dedicated florists. We understand how important it is to stand out in a creative yet interesting way. Coming up with new ideas and improving our services are just a few of our goals and we try to maintain the beauty and purity of flowers with each step we take.

With each wedding plan, we are constantly making sure the flowers are delivered in the best conditions, so you can enjoy them for the longest time. We believe sustainability is key, which means we avoid plastic wraps, as well as using artificial flowers. The important part in taking care of the flowers is watering them with rain water, which extends their lifespan and keeps them thriving.

Our passion and interest for the industry brought us local partners, who offered us partnership and gave us the opportunity to grow as a company. Every flower we use in our atelier comes from the local farm Burina, where no chemicals are used so the flowers can last longer, grow pure and beautiful. And because our focus is the customer’s joy, Laura bakery partnered with us in the process and now she is providing us with sweets, for extra smiles.
